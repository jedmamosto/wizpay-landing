# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Visitors, prospective PH merchants, developers exploring Philippine payment configurations.

## Product Purpose
Static marketing showcase of WizPay's open-source direct payment checkout overlays.

## Brand Personality
Modern, vibrant, professional, developer-friendly.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS platforms, cream/neutral templates, flat designs.

## Design Principles
- **Direct integrations**: Direct connection to APIs and overlay checkouts.
- **Fee-free accessibility**: Open-source, zero-fee direct payment layers.
- **High mobile conversion**: Fast-loading, responsive screens designed for mobile checkouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Focus on fast loading times for mobile networks (3G/4G) in the Philippines.
- High color contrast for mobile screens under direct sunlight.
- Keyboard navigability for checkout steps.
